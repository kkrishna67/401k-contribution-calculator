# 401(k) Contribution Calculator

A single-page calculator for determining the exact contribution percentages to enter in your 401(k) plan portal (e.g. Fidelity NetBenefits) to hit your contribution goals for the year.

## What It Does

Given your salary, pay schedule, and year-to-date contributions, the calculator tells you:

- **Match Contribution %** — the percentage to enter in your plan portal to capture your full employer match over your remaining pay periods
- **Catch-up Contribution %** — if you're eligible (age 50+), the percentage needed to hit the catch-up limit over your remaining pay periods
- **Contribution per Pay Period** — the exact dollar amount per paycheck for each

It accounts for contributions already made this year so the percentages stay accurate mid-year.

## Features

- 2026 IRS contribution limits ($23,500 elective deferral, $8,000 standard catch-up, $11,250 SECURE 2.0 super catch-up for ages 60–63)
- Employer match calculator — shows max match, automatic contribution, and total employer contribution
- Estimated federal tax savings based on your marginal rate
- Three contribution goals: Capture Full Match, Maximize Contributions, or Custom Target
- YTD progress tracking for pre-tax basic and Roth catch-up contributions
- Persistent inputs — all settings saved automatically in your browser (except salary)
- Links directly to the IRS 401(k) limits page

## Usage

Open `index.html` in any browser. No installation or dependencies required.

1. Enter your **Total Annual Salary** and **Total Pay Periods**
2. Set your **401(k) limits** and **catch-up eligibility**
3. Enter your **employer match** details
4. Select your **contribution goal**
5. Update **YTD contributions** (pre-tax and Roth if you have it)and **pay periods remaining** as the year progresses

The contribution percentages update instantly as you type.
